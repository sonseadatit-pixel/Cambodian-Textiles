Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CTW textile manufacturing and global shipping services.

What products does CTW manufacture?

CTW specializes in textile and uniform products such as lab coats, shop coats, medical uniforms, industrial garments, and fabric materials for global customers.

How do you ensure product quality?

CTW maintains strict quality control through single-source manufacturing professional inspection and international production standards to ensure consistent quality in every order.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, CTW ships products globally including the USA, Canada, UK, Ireland, and other international markets with reliable logistics and fast delivery.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, CTW provides product and fabric samples so customers can check quality and materials before placing bulk orders.

What is your minimum order quantity?

CTW offers flexible order quantities depending on product type and customer requirements, making it easier for businesses to start.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping time depends on destination. Normally:
USA & Canada: 7-15 days
UK & Ireland: 10-18 days
Other countries: based on shipping method
