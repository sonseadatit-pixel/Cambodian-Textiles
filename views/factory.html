Our Quality Assurance Process

01
Material Selection

Strict checking of incoming yarns and fabrics for structural weight (GSM), color shade accuracy, and optimal shrink threshold control.

02
In-Line QC

Constant inspection checkpoints directly on our sewing lines to immediately test seams, panel alignment, and stitch density.

03
Final Inspection

Every single completed item (Scrubs, Chef Coats, Hotel Linens) passes through a complete manual aesthetic review prior to technical steam pressing.

04
Secure Packaging

Moisture-proof polypropylene internal bags layered within premium outer heavy-ply shipping cartons to preserve quality across ocean freight lanes.
